Overview

  • Task: Start a list of ten professional contacts.
  • Purpose: Identify and pursue networking and work opportunities in your area.

Instructions

  1. Study the lesson called Professional Contact List Links to an external site..
  2. Use the template called Professional Contact List Links to an external site. to create your list. You will add to this spreadsheet this week and during the course. See the sample Download sample for an example of how this can be done.
    • You must at least have a name and some contact information on each row.
    • Contact information can include things like the following a phone number, WhatsApp number, email address, street address, FaceBook profile address, and so forth. 
  3. You will be graded on having at least ten contacts.
  4. Submit your Professional Contact List spreadsheet.
